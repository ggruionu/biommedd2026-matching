Morning-of-conference setup (2 minutes)

  1. Go to dropbox.com/developers/apps and click your Dropbox app.
  2. Click the Settings tab. Under OAuth 2 click Generate. Copy the token.
  3. Go to your GitHub repo and click index.html.
  4. Click the pencil icon. Press Ctrl+F, search for YOUR_TOKEN_HERE.
  5. Replace YOUR_TOKEN_HERE with the token (keep the single quotes).
  6. Click Commit changes. Wait 60 seconds then check admin.

Token lasts 4 hours. Regenerate if needed between sessions.
